<h4>SQL</h4>

```sql
with cte as (select cinema.*, 
    lag(free) over(order by seat_id) as prev_seat,
    lead(free) over(order by seat_id) as next_seat
    from cinema)
select SEAT_ID from cte where FREE=1 and (prev_seat=1 or next_seat=1)
```
